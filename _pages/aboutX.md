I'm a second-year master's student at the Department of Artificial Intelligence, College of Informatics, Korea University. I'm fortunate to work under the tutelage of [Prof. Anh Tong](https://anh-tong.github.io/) at [SDML lab](https://sdml-ku.github.io/).

Before coming to Korea University and joining SDML lab, I earned my B.Eng. in Control Engineering and Automation from Hanoi University of Science and Technology in Vietnam. 

My research interests lie at the intersection of deep learning and large foundation models, with a specific focus on parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT) and model optimization dynamics. I am also eager to explore interdisciplinary domains to broaden my knowledge horizons and tackle scalable AI challenges.
